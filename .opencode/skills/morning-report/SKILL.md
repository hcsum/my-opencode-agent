---
name: morning-report
description: Use this skill whenever the user wants to generate a morning report, daily briefing, or start-of-day summary. Triggers include: "morning report", "daily brief", "what's on my plate today", "summarize my day", or requests to compile calendar events, emails, tasks, and news into a single structured digest. Also use when the user wants to automate or schedule a recurring daily summary. Do NOT use for single-source summaries (e.g. "summarize my emails only"), weekly reports, or ad-hoc research tasks unrelated to a daily briefing format.
---

## What does the report include

- Summerize user's favorite news sources update
- Provide news about user's stock holdings and performance
- List user's todos for the day
